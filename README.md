A lightweight **price scraper** that extracts product prices from competitor websites and compares them in real time. Perfect for competitive analysis and market monitoring.

---

## Features

- **Multi-site scraping** – Pull prices from multiple competitor pages
- **Real-time price extraction** – Get up-to-date pricing instantly
- **Price comparison engine** – Compare competitor prices side-by-side with yours
